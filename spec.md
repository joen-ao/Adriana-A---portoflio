## 1. Visual Identity & Mood
* **Concept:** "Quiet Luxury" meets "Global Business." The site must look like a high-end editorial magazine (e.g., Vogue Business or Kinfolk).
* **Palette:** Creams, Off-Whites, Deep Forest Green, and Charcoal Black.
* **Typography:** * *Headings:* Classic Serif (e.g., Playfair Display, Cinzel, or Bodoni).
    * *Body:* Minimalist Sans-Serif (e.g., Montserrat, Lato, or Inter).
* **Atmosphere:** Professional, cinematic, high-definition, and spacious (heavy use of negative space).
